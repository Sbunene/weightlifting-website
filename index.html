About My Hobby

I started weightlifting about a year ago. At first it was hard, but now I really enjoy it. I go to the gym three times a week.

Weightlifting is not just about getting strong, it's also about discipline and consistency.

Benefits of Weightlifting

I've noticed many benefits since I started weightlifting:

Physical Benefits

I feel stronger and have more energy throughout the day. My posture has improved too.

Mental Benefits

Weightlifting helps me reduce stress and feel more confident. It's a great way to clear my mind.

My Workout Routine

Here is my simple 3-day workout routine:

  • Monday: Chest and Triceps
  • Wednesday: Back and Biceps
  • Friday: Legs and Shoulders

Tips for Beginners

If you're new to weightlifting, here's what I've learned:

Start Light

Focus on proper form rather than heavy weights. It's better to lift light with good technique.

Be Consistent

Try to stick to a regular schedule. Consistency is more important than intensity.

My Progress

When I started, I could only bench press 25kg. Now I can lift 45kg! It's not a lot compared to some people, but I'm proud of my progress.
